About

Sabato Nocera started his Ph.D. in Computer Science at the University of Salerno, Italy, in November 2022. He previously earned his Master’s and Bachelor’s degrees in Computer Science from the same university in September 2022 and 2020, respectively. Since September 2024, he has also been a Research Fellow, contributing to the project Mining Software Repositories for enhanced Software Bills of Materials (MSR4SBOM). His research interests include Empirical Software Engineering and Software Analytics.
